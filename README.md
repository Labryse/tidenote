# TideNote

A note-taking app that brings writing and drawing into one workspace. Write in blocks, sketch on an infinite canvas, and weave your notes together with links.

**Web:** https://tidenote.app
**Download:** Windows — [latest release](https://github.com/Labryse/tidenote/releases/latest) · _(macOS, Linux, Android, iOS coming soon)_

---

## What makes it different

Most apps make you choose: a document editor _or_ a whiteboard. TideNote is both, in the same space — built for people who think in text and visuals at once.

- **Block-based editor** — headings, tables, collections, and rich content blocks
- **Infinite canvas** — combine freehand drawing, images, and shapes alongside text
- **Linked notes** — connect ideas with in-note `[[links]]` to build a web of knowledge
- **Organize your way** — folders, color labels, and full-text search across everything
